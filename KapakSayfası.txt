THE INTERNET.
IT IS COOL, FAST AND GLOBAL.
But it is also complex. Sometimes it can
be difficult to see where we are at risk–
and where our private stuff online can
be read and used by others. Luckily, in
our parallel universe where the DIGITAL
DEFENDERS fight the evil DATA INTRUD-
ERS, it is easy to see who are the good
and who are the bad guys.
